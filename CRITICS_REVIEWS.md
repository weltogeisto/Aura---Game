# Critic's Reviews — Complete Voiceover Script

## Design Notes

The Critic is an unseen voice—aristocratic, world-weary, simultaneously impressed and disappointed. Think: John Malkovich reading Werner Herzog's diary.

Every shot receives a review. The Critic sees everything. The Critic judges.

---

## Universal Reviews (Any Scenario)

### Miss (Hit Nothing of Value)

- *"The wall. You shot the wall. The wall is... fine, actually. It's seen worse."*
- *"Air. You hit air. Air is free. Congratulations on your economy."*
- *"A purely pedestrian effort. The drywall union sends its regards."*

### Graze (Partial Hit)

- *"A flesh wound. The conservators will have opinions."*
- *"You have... annoyed the artifact. It will recover. Will you?"*

### Low-Value Target

- *"An interesting choice. The janitor will be cross."*
- *"Some would call this a statement. I call it a waste of ammunition."*

### High-Value Target (Clean Hit)

- *"Adequate. The insurance adjusters weep."*
- *"History will remember this moment. History will not be kind."*

### Perfect Shot (Maximum Damage)

- *"Exquisite. Horrifying. I cannot look away."*
- *"You have understood the assignment. I wish you hadn't."*

## Special Condition Reviews

### Player Takes Too Long to Shoot

- *"The crosshair trembles. The art waits. Neither of you is getting younger."*
- *"Hesitation. The mark of either wisdom or cowardice. The art cannot tell the difference."*

### Player Shoots Immediately (Under 2 Seconds)

- *"Impulsive. Raw. Uncontaminated by thought. The Dadaists would approve."*
- *"No consideration. No remorse. You would have made an excellent critic."*

### Player Shoots Multiple Times (Game Error)

- *"One shot. You had one shot. The rules are simple. You are not."*

### Player Quits Before Shooting

- *"You walked away. The art remains intact. Was this mercy, or cowardice? History will not record either."*
