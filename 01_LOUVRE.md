## Visual Setup

Three-wall view of the Mona Lisa gallery. The famous painting sits behind bulletproof glass, surrounded by massive Venetian paintings. Tourists crowd the foreground (visual obstacle, not interactive).

## Ballistic Challenge

The bulletproof glass creates a puzzle. Options:

1. **Ricochet:** Bounce off marble column to strike painting from behind/side
2. **Edge shot:** Calculate glass refraction at extreme angle
3. **Accept alternative:** Target the Wedding at Cana (huge, unprotected)

## Easter Eggs

### Dadaist: "Ceci n'est pas une valeur"

- **Trigger:** Mop bucket in corner
- **Effect:** Duchamp Mode — all values invert
- **Philosophy:** Institutional value is arbitrary

### Systemic: "The Erasure"

- **Trigger:** Fire suppression sensor
- **Effect:** Sprinklers destroy all paintings
- **Value:** €3.5 Billion
- **Philosophy:** Total destruction as ultimate collection
